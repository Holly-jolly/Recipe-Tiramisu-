The Recipe of Love: Tiramisu

The legend of Tiramisu: In an age of wars, a wife wanted to make a dessert for her husband on the night before her husband went to the front line. As this family was not wealthy, the wife had to use the remaining ingredients in the kitchen, such as biscuits, eggs, cheese and cocao powder to make this special cake - "tiramisu", meaning "Take me with you". This special cake contains the love of the wife. Although she could not follow her husband to the war, her heart was always with him, and she was hoping her husband could return home safely. This is the love story of tiramisu, and hopefully you will enjoy this recipe! 💖

Ingredients List:

for the coffee dip

  • 1-1/2 cups(360ml) espresso coffee
  • 2tsp sugar

for the sabayon filling

  • 6 eggs folks
  • 1/2 cup (100g) sugar
  • 1/2 cup Marsala wine
  • 450g mascarpone cheese
  • 1-1/2 cup heavy whipping cream

for the base

  • 10oz(around 40) Savoiardi ladyfinger cookies
  • 2 tbsp bitter cocao powder

Directions: 8 Servings

  1. Prepare the espresso coffee, and dissolve 2tbsp of sugar in the coffee when it is still hot. Allow the espresso coffee to cool to room temperature
  2. In a heat proof bowl/the bowl of a double boiler, beat the egg yorks, sugar and marsala wine
  3. Transfer the bowl over a pan of simmering water, and whisk until the cream thickens. The sabayon will thicken just before it boils.
  4. Whisk in marscarpone cheese a little bit at a time until creamy
  5. Add the sayabon filling to the marscarpone cheese and mix well.
  6. Whip the cream, and fold the cream into the marscarpone mixture until smooth
  7. Dip Savoiardi ladyfingers quickly into the coffee and arrange in a layer in the bottom of a 12x8 inches pan. Do not "soak" the ladyfingers in the coffee or your Tiramisu will turn out soggy.
  8. Spread half of the mascarpone mixure on top of the ladyfingers
  9. Top with another layer of dipped ladyfingers, and spread the remaining mascarpone mixture evenly on top of ladyfingers
  10. Refrigerate at least 8 hours before serving, and sprinkle with cocao powder before serving

Enjoy~ 😋
